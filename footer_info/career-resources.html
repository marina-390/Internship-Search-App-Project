📝 Resume Writing Guide

Create a professional resume that stands out to employers and passes ATS systems.

  • Use clear structure and formatting
  • Focus on achievements, not duties
  • Use strong action verbs
  • Keep it 1 page for internships

Write personalized cover letters that increase your chances of getting interviews.

  • Keep it short and clear
  • Avoid generic templates
