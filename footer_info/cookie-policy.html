Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help remember information about your visit and preferences.

How We Use Cookies

InternHub uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (login and authentication)
  • Preference Cookies: Store your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Marketing Cookies: Track advertising performance and user interests

Types of Cookies

Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They are used to maintain your session and authentication.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a longer period. We use them to remember your preferences and login information.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies for analytics and advertising purposes.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept all cookies
  • Set preferences by cookie type
  • Delete cookies after your session

Your Choices

If you disable cookies, some features of InternHub may not work properly. We recommend keeping essential cookies enabled for the best experience.
