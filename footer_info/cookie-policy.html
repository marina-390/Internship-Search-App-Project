Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember information about your session and preferences.

Cookies We Use

InternHub uses only essential cookies. We do not use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies.

Storage Key Purpose Duration
isLoggedIn Remembers that you are logged in Until logout
userId Stores your user ID for session management Until logout
userRole Stores your account type (student or company) Until logout
cookieConsent Remembers your cookie consent choice Persistent

These values are stored in your browser's localStorage. They are necessary for the platform to function and do not require consent under GDPR (ePrivacy Directive Art. 5(3)).

Managing Your Data

You can clear all stored data at any time by:

  • Logging out of your account
  • Clearing localStorage and cookies in your browser settings
  • Deleting your account via the "Danger Zone" in your profile
