Interview Tips & Preparation

Master your internship interviews with expert guidance

Before Interview

Research the Company

Learn about mission, values, and products before the interview.

Practice Questions

Prepare answers like "Tell me about yourself".

Prepare Stories

Use STAR method: Situation, Task, Action, Result.

Mock Interviews

Practice with friends or mentors.

During Interview

First Impression

Be on time, dress well, and stay confident.

Communication

Listen carefully and answer clearly.

Show Enthusiasm

Explain why you want this role.

Ask Questions

Ask about team and company culture.

After Interview

Thank You Email

Send within 24 hours.

Self Review

Improve your future performance.

Follow Up

Check status politely if needed.

Negotiation

Focus on learning opportunities and growth.

Common Interview Questions

  • Tell me about yourself
  • Why this internship?
  • Strengths and weaknesses
  • Challenge you faced
  • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
