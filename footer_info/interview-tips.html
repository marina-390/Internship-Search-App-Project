Interview Tips & Preparation

Master your internship interviews with expert guidance

Before Your Interview

Research the Company

Learn about the company's mission, values, recent news, and products. This shows genuine interest and helps you ask informed questions.

Practice Common Questions

Prepare answers for questions like "Tell me about yourself" and "Why are you interested in this internship?"

Prepare Your Stories

Have 2-3 compelling stories ready that demonstrate your skills and experience using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result).

Mock Interviews

Practice with a friend or mentor to build confidence and get feedback on your responses.

During Your Interview

First Impressions Matter

Arrive on time, dress appropriately, make eye contact, and give a firm handshake. A positive first impression sets the tone.

Listen and Engage

Listen carefully to each question, pause before answering, and ask clarifying questions if needed.

Showcase Your Enthusiasm

Express genuine interest in the role and the company. Share why this opportunity excites you.

Ask Thoughtful Questions

Prepare questions about the team, company culture, and what success looks like in the role.

After Your Interview

Send a Thank You Email

Within 24 hours, send a brief thank-you email mentioning specific points from the conversation.

Reflect on Your Performance

Think about what went well and what you could improve for future interviews.

Follow Up Appropriately

If you haven't heard back within the expected timeframe, follow up politely to check on your application status.

Negotiate Wisely

If you receive an offer, negotiate respectfully. Focus on the overall package, including learning opportunities.

Common Interview Questions

  • Tell me about yourself
  • Why did you choose this internship?
  • What are your strengths?
  • What is your biggest weakness?
  • Describe a challenge you overcame
  • Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
  • Do you have any questions for us?
