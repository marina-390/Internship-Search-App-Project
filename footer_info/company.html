How the hiring system works on InternHub

📌 How posting a job works

On InternHub, companies do not directly search for students. Instead, they create internship offers that students can apply to.

  • Companies publish internship opportunities
  • Students browse available jobs
  • Students apply by sending their CV and details
  • Companies receive applications only after students apply

👨‍💼 What companies can do

  • Create internship listings
  • Receive student applications
  • Review CVs and skills
  • Contact selected candidates

🔒 What companies cannot do

  • Browse all students freely
  • See private student profiles
  • Access data without application

⚙️ System logic

The system is based on applications. A student applies first, then the company can see their information in the application list.

💡 Summary

InternHub is an application-based hiring platform. Companies receive talent only through student applications, ensuring privacy and structured recruitment.
