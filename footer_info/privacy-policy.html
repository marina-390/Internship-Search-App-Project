2. What Data We Collect

Data you provide directly

  • Account details: name, email address, password (stored as a hash)
  • Student profile: first name, last name, phone number, city, education, skills, about me
  • Company profile: company name, business ID (Y-tunnus), contact email, website, city, description
  • Uploaded files: CV/resume (PDF), profile photo
  • Application data: cover letters, internship applications

Data collected automatically

  • Browser type and device information
  • Session cookies required for login functionality

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)): processing necessary to provide our internship matching service
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): for optional features and marketing communications, which you may withdraw at any time
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): for improving service quality and preventing fraud
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): where required by Finnish or EU law

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match students with internship opportunities
  • To allow companies to review applications
  • To send transactional emails (application confirmations, interview invitations)
  • To improve the platform

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase Inc. — our database and authentication provider (servers in EU region). Data Processing Agreement in place.
  • EmailJS — used to send transactional emails. Only the data necessary for sending the email is transmitted.
  • Companies on our platform — when you submit an internship application, your profile data and CV are shared with the relevant employer.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Upon account deletion, personal data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Uploaded CVs and photos are deleted immediately upon account deletion
  • Application records may be retained for up to 12 months for legal compliance

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As an EU resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: correct inaccurate data via your profile settings
  • Right to erasure: delete your account and all associated data via the "Danger Zone" section in your profile
  • Right to data portability: request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object: object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent: at any time, without affecting prior processing

8. Cookies

We use only essential session cookies required for login functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. For more details, see our Cookie Policy.

9. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and role-based access controls. However, no system is 100% secure.

10. Supervisory Authority

If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (Tietosuojavaltuutettu):
Website: tietosuoja.fi
Email: tietosuoja@om.fi
