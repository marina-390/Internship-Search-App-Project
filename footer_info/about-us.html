About InternHub

Empowering students to find meaningful internship opportunities

Our Mission

InternHub is a student-built project created by three students with a simple goal: to help other students find internships more easily during challenging times. We aim to connect students with companies that offer real opportunities to learn, grow, and gain experience.

Our Vision

To build a platform where every student can easily access internship opportunities that support their personal and professional growth.

What We Offer

  • Simple internship search platform
  • Career preparation tools
  • Connections with companies
  • CV and interview resources
  • Student support system

Why InternHub?

🎯 Reliable Opportunities

We focus on sharing useful and relevant internship listings.

🚀 Career Development

We help students gain skills and prepare for their future careers.

💼 Real Connections

We connect students directly with companies and professionals.

📱 Simple Experience

Our platform is clean, fast, and easy to use for everyone.
