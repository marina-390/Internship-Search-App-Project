Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a job on InternHub?

Simply sign up as an employer, complete your company profile, and click "Post a Job". Follow our step-by-step form to create and publish your internship posting.

Can I edit my job posting after publishing?

Yes, you can edit your job posting at any time from your company dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.

How do students apply for internships?

Students can browse internships on our platform, click on a job listing, and submit their application with their resume and cover letter.

Is there a fee to use InternHub as a student?

No, InternHub is completely free for students. Create an account, build your profile, and start applying for internships today!
