Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for an internship?

You can browse available internships, open a listing, and submit your application with your resume.

Is InternHub free?

Yes, InternHub is completely free for students.

Can I edit my profile later?

Yes, you can update your profile anytime after logging in.

How do employers see my CV?

When you apply for an internship, employers can view your uploaded CV and profile details.
